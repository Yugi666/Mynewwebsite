ABOUT THE PROJECT

Yummy is a project that aims to revolutionize the classic Gofundme by allowing the contributors to also obtain an investor status in the business. Yummy will utilize NFT tech to create a new form of investment in the restaurant industry. We plan to continually increase the value of the NFT over the starting years so as to fulfill our obligations to investors by year end. This work will ensure that we can maintain our majority shares and support a passive income for the years to follow. A passive income may offer new avenues for investment. Providing its continued growth, we intend to expand Yummy into an Italian street-style food chain.

Brick-and-mortars have become established as a safe investment for traders and in recent years, NFT. With our Knowledge of NFT and our vast experience in the food industry, we are certain that in four years we will be able to fully achieve our goals: the realization of the new $Yummy coin and an expanding restaurant chain that competes with the brick. We are confident that at the end of the 4 years we will have an operating system to create our vision for the stock market. We will have dividends from the restaurant investments and above all, our NFT will have a higher value with the growth of the company. Our only ask is that you continue to believe in the longevity of this project and evaluate the NFT for its intrinstic value to bring the restaurant industry into the future.
